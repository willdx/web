5.字典和集合

dict和set

字典

基本操作

In [11]: dic = { "a": "a", "b": "b", "c": "c"}
    ...:

In [12]: dic = { "a": "a", "b": "b", "c": "c"}

In [13]: dic["a"]
Out[13]: 'a'

In [14]: dic["a"] = "aa"

In [15]: del dic["a"]

In [16]: dic
Out[16]: {'b': 'b', 'c': 'c'}

In [17]: dic2 = {"d": "d"}

In [18]: dic3 = dict(dic, **dic2)

In [19]: dic3
Out[19]: {'b': 'b', 'c': 'c', 'd': 'd'}

In [20]: len(dic)
Out[20]: 2

循环

dic = { "a": "a", "b": "b", "c": "c"}
for key, value in dic.items();
    print("key:{}, value:{}".format(key, value))

生成式

In [21]: a = {str(k): v for k, v in enumerate(range(3))}

In [22]: a
Out[22]: {'0': 0, '1': 1, '2': 2}

集合

记得数学中学的集合么?

集合的元素不允许重复

In [23]: _s = set()

In [24]: _s.add(1)

In [25]: _s.add(1)

In [26]: len(_s)
Out[26]: 1

集合的交/差/并/异或

In [27]: _s
Out[27]: {1}

In [28]: _b = {1, 2, 3}

In [29]: _s.intersection(_b)
Out[29]: {1}

In [30]: _s.union(_b)
Out[30]: {1, 2, 3}

In [31]: _s.difference(_b)
Out[31]: set()

In [32]: _b.difference(_s)
Out[32]: {2, 3}

In [33]: _s.symmetric_difference(_b)
Out[33]: {2, 3}
